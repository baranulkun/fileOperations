Course Content: Vectors in 3D space; Lines and planes; Coordinate systems and transformations; Vector valued functions, Functions of several variables: Limits, Continuity, Partial Derivative, Chain Rule, Directional Derivative, Differential; Maximum-Minimum problems, Lagrange multipliers; Multiple integrals and applications; Line integrals and surface integrals. This course is a continuation  [[Mathematics I]].
